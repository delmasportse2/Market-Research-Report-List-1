<p><strong>The Cosmetics and Personal Care Emulsifier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil-in-water (o/w) Emulsifier</li><li>Water-in-oil (w/o) Emulsifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cosmetics and Personal Care Emulsifier Market is primarily divided into two types: oil-in-water (o/w) and water-in-oil (w/o) emulsifiers. O/W emulsifiers are used to create lightweight formulations, where oil droplets are dispersed in water, making products like lotions and creams easily spreadable and suitable for oily skin types. Conversely, W/O emulsifiers encapsulate water droplets in oil, resulting in thicker, richer formulations ideal for dry or sensitive skin, providing enhanced moisture retention and barrier protection.</p></p>
