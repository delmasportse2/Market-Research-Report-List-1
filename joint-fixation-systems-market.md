<p><p>The Joint Fixation Systems market comprises various components such as plates, screws, rods, pins, and wires, each serving specific orthopedic or surgical purposes. Plates provide stability and support for fractured bones, while screws secure these plates or directly stabilize bones. Rods are used in intramedullary fixation, providing internal support for long bones. Pins are employed for precise alignment in fractures, and wires are often utilized for tensioning or stabilization in complex repairs, facilitating efficient healing and rehabilitation.</p></p>
