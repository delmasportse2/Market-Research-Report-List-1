<p><p>The fully-automatic capsule filling machine market includes mechanical vibration filling machines and compression filling machines. Mechanical vibration filling machines utilize vibrations to efficiently distribute powder into capsules, ensuring uniformity and minimizing waste. In contrast, compression filling machines use a pressing mechanism to compact the powder into capsules, offering higher density and reduced air content. Both types enhance productivity and precision in pharmaceuticals and nutraceuticals, catering to diverse manufacturing needs while ensuring high-quality capsules.</p></p>
