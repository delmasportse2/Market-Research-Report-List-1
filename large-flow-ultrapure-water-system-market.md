<p><p>The Large Flow Ultrapure Water System market encompasses systems designed for high-purity water production, primarily using Reverse Osmosis (RO) combined with Ion Exchange (IX) or Electrodeionization (EDI). The RO+IX method effectively removes contaminants and minerals, ensuring water quality, while IX provides further purification by exchanging ions. Conversely, RO+EDI systems utilize a continuous deionization process, enhancing efficiency and reducing chemical usage. Both configurations cater to industries like pharmaceuticals and electronics, where ultrapure water is critical for processes and product quality.</p></p>
