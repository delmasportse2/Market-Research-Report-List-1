<p><p>Electronic Parts Catalog (EPC) software is categorized into two main market types: On Cloud and On Premise. </p><p>On Cloud EPC solutions are hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, offering scalability, lower upfront costs, and ease of updates. This model is ideal for businesses seeking flexibility and remote access.</p><p>On Premise EPC software is installed locally on a company’s servers, providing direct control over data and system configurations.
