<p><p>The amniotic product market consists of three main types: cryopreserved amniotic membranes, dehydrated amniotic membranes, and amniotic suspensions. Cryopreserved amniotic membranes retain their biological properties and are used in various surgical applications, offering advantages like reduced immune response. Dehydrated amniotic membranes are shelf-stable and convenient for use in wound healing and reconstructive surgeries. Amniotic suspensions, typically containing amniotic cells and growth factors, are utilized in regenerative medicine to promote healing and tissue repair. Each type serves distinct clinical needs and applications.</p></p>
