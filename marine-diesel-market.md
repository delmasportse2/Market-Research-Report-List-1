<p><p>The marine diesel market primarily comprises two types: Light Diesel Oil (LDO) and Heavy-Duty Diesel Oil (HDDO). LDO is typically used in smaller vessels and offers better ignition and combustion efficiency, making it suitable for lower load applications. In contrast, HDDO is designed for larger ships, equipped to handle high-load operations and delivering greater fuel efficiency and stability under heavy conditions. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining the operational efficiency and environmental compliance of marine transportation.</p></p>
