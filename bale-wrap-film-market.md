<p><p>Bale wrap film is primarily categorized into three types: LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene), LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene), and others. LLDPE is known for its superior strength and stretchability, making it ideal for wrapping bales securely. LDPE, while less stretchy, offers good puncture resistance and is often used for lighter applications. Other materials may include specialty films catering to specific needs, such as moisture resistance or UV stability, providing various options for farmers and users in diverse agricultural settings.</p></p>
