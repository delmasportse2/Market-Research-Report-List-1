<p><ul><li>Beside-the-press Granulators</li><li>Central Granulators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The waste granulator market typically features two main types: beside-the-press granulators and central granulators. Beside-the-press granulators are compact units placed directly next to injection molding or extrusion machines, facilitating immediate material processing and enhancing production efficiency. In contrast, central granulators are larger systems designed for processing significant volumes of waste from multiple machinery or production lines, focusing on high-capacity applications and bulk material handling. Both types are essential for optimizing recycling and waste reduction in manufacturing.</p></p>
