<p><p>Enterprise Asset Management (EAM) systems can be categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based EAM systems are hosted on remote servers, offering scalability, accessibility, and reduced upfront costs. They typically provide regular updates and require minimal IT infrastructure. In contrast, on-premises EAM systems are installed locally on a company's servers, offering greater control and customization but necessitating higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Businesses choose between these options based on their specific needs and resources.</p></p>
