<p><p>The children's life vest market consists of three primary types: foam vests, inflatable vests, and hybrid vests. Foam vests are made from buoyant materials, providing consistent flotation, making them ideal for younger kids. Inflatable vests, which can be manually or automatically inflated, are lightweight and compact but require proper operation. Hybrid vests combine foam and inflatable elements for versatility, offering adjustable buoyancy and comfort. Each type caters to different needs based on activity level, age, and water conditions.</p></p>
