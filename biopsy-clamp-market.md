<p><p>The biopsy clamp market is categorized into two main types: rigid biopsy clamps and flexible biopsy clamps. Rigid biopsy clamps are designed for stable tissue sampling, providing precision and control during the biopsy procedure, often used in areas requiring firm tissue access. In contrast, flexible biopsy clamps offer adaptability to various anatomical shapes, allowing for effective sampling in more delicate or complex regions. Both types serve critical roles in diagnostics, enhancing the accuracy of tissue acquisition in medical practice.</p></p>
