<p><strong>The Alcohol Breath Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fuel Cell</li><li>Semiconductor</li><li>Infrared</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The alcohol breath analyzer market comprises three main types: fuel cell, semiconductor, and infrared sensors. Fuel cell analyzers use electrochemical reactions to measure alcohol levels, offering high accuracy and fast results, commonly used in law enforcement. Semiconductor analyzers rely on resistance changes in materials when exposed to alcohol, making them compact and cost-effective but less precise. Infrared analyzers measure alcohol concentration by detecting specific light absorption, providing high sensitivity and accurate results, often employed in industrial and medical applications.</p></p>
